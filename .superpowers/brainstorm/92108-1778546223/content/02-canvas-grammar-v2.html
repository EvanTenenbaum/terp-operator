Section 2 (v2) — Canvas grammar, slimmed

Quick Start collapsed into the Keel (chips are nav, not forms). Identity ribbon is one slim line. The Context Drawer absorbs everything else that used to live in always-on bands.

What changed
v1 — 5 zones, two always-visible bands
  • Zone A Keel + Zone B Quick Start are two separate strips, ~88px combined.
  • Quick Start has inline fields per lane (Client, Request, Vendor, Expected, Amount, Method…).
  • Persistent context band shows Bal · Credit · Tags · 3 buttons.
  • Selection context strip lives above the grid.
v2 — 4 zones, one keel strip
  • Quick Start absorbed into the Keel as 5 nav-only chips. No inline fields anywhere global.
  • Keel is one 48px strip total.
  • Identity ribbon is a slim 28px line, only when you're inside an entity.
  • Everything formerly in always-on control bands → Drawer tabs.

Keel chips — pure navigation, no inline forms
ChipWhat it does
SaleGo to Sales. If a customer is in recent context, restore it. Otherwise land on customer-pick state. No client/request fields in the chip.
ReceiveGo to Intake. Pre-selection strip on the page has Vendor ▾ and "+ Receive Row".
$ InGo to Payments with the Quick Ledger focused on a fresh money_in row.
$ OutGo to Vendor Payouts. Pre-selection: Vendor ▾.
PurchaseGo to Purchase Orders. Pre-selection: Vendor ▾ and "+ New PO".
"Start a customer-aware sale from anywhere" remains possible via ⌘K Rich Star → routes to Sales with that customer active. Or click Sale chip and pick on the Sales view. The all-in-one quick-launch form is gone.
