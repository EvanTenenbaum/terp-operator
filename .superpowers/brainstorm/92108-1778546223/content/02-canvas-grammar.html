Focus mode — revised
Today (problem)
  • WorkspacePanel focus mode hides ALL siblings via hiddenByFocus.
  • QuickStartBar returns null when focusedPanelId is set.
  • Operator loses orientation, can't start new work without exiting focus.
Revised
  • Zone A (Keel) survives focus. ⌘K + 4 launch icons + health stay.
  • Zone B (Quick Start) collapses to a one-line breadcrumb but the chips remain clickable.
  • Zone C (persistent context band) survives — you're still inside the customer/vendor/PO.
  • Zone E (Context Drawer) follows the focused panel — its tabs still address the active entity.
  • Only Zone D (the focused grid) expands. Sibling Zone D panels (e.g. PO lines vs PO grid) collapse to header-only.
  • Esc exits focus and restores.
